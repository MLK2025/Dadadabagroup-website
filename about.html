Who we are

Dadaba Group is a diversified investment company advancing infrastructure, energy, real estate and agritech with a commitment to inclusive growth.

Our vision & approach

We partner with governments, development agencies and entrepreneurs to deliver resilient assets and transformative services. Our model blends disciplined capital allocation with deep community engagement and measurable ESG outcomes.

Our Journey

From a single enterprise to a multi-sector group—guided by purpose and partnership.

2018 — Roots Founder launches the first ventures focused on property and talent development.
2020 — Expansion New portfolio companies in media and recruitment scale across West Africa.
2022 — ESG Commitment Group-wide sustainability framework established with board oversight.
2023 — Carbon & Agritech Carbon Hub and Smart Farming launched to accelerate climate action and food security.
2025 — Integrated Platform Consolidated capabilities in infrastructure, energy and real estate to drive inclusive growth.
