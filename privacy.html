Scope & Controller

This Privacy Policy explains how Dadaba Group and its subsidiaries (“Dadaba”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collect and process personal data through our websites, events and communications (the “Services”).

Controller: Dadaba Group, Head Office — East Legon, Boundary Rd, Accra, Ghana. Email: privacy@dadabagroup.com.

Note: This page complements our Privacy section and Cookie Policy. If a subsidiary presents a product with its own policy, that policy will govern that product.

Data We Collect

  • Contact data: name, email, phone, organization, role.
  • Account & preferences: newsletter choices, saved settings.
  • Usage & device data: pages viewed, interactions, approximate location, IP address, identifiers, browser/device info, diagnostics.
  • Careers data: CV/resume, work history, education, references, right-to-work information (where lawful).
  • Partner/Investor data: business contact details, due diligence information provided lawfully.
  • Content you share: messages, enquiries, feedback.

We avoid collecting sensitive data unless strictly necessary and lawful (e.g., health or biometric data). Do not include sensitive information in open forms unless requested.

How We Collect

  • Directly from you (forms, emails, events, applications).
  • Automatically via cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies).
  • From third parties (recruiters, references, public sources, service providers), where lawful.

How We Use Data

  • Provide, operate and improve our Services and sites.
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer, partner and investor support.
  • Recruitment and HR processing for candidates.
  • Communications, newsletters and event invitations (you can opt out).
  • Security, fraud prevention and compliance with law.
  • Analytics to understand usage and enhance content and accessibility.

Legal Bases

Depending on your location, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent (e.g., newsletters, certain cookies).
  • Contract (to perform a contract or take steps at your request).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., site operation, security, communications) balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligations (e.g., record-keeping, regulatory requests).

Sharing & International Transfers

  • Subsidiaries & affiliates for group operations consistent with this Policy.
  • Service providers (IT hosting, analytics, HR tools, communications) under contract and confidentiality.
  • Professional advisers (legal, audit, insurance) where necessary.
  • Legal & safety to comply with law or defend rights.
  • Business transfers in connection with mergers, acquisitions or reorganization.

Where data is transferred internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards (e.g., contractual clauses) consistent with applicable law.

Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, to comply with legal/contractual duties, or to resolve disputes. When no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize data.

Your Rights

Your rights depend on your jurisdiction and may include:

  • Access your data and request a copy.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete data in certain circumstances.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Portability of data you provided in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

Ghana: We process data in line with the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) principles.

EU/UK: If you are in the EEA/UK, you may contact your supervisory authority and have additional rights under GDPR/UK GDPR.

California: We do not sell personal information. To limit sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising, adjust Cookie preferences.

To exercise rights, see Data Request below or email privacy@dadabagroup.com.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use necessary cookies to run the site and (with your consent) analytics/functional cookies to improve content. You can change preferences at any time via Cookie Policy & Preferences or your browser settings.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risks (access controls, encryption in transit, backups). No system is 100% secure; please notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized activity.

Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
