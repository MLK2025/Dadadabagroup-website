Good to Know

FAQ

Who is eligible for scholarships?

Basic to tertiary students from partner districts. We prioritise girls in STEM and learners from low‑income households. Proof of need and school validation are required.

Do you fund building new facilities?

We focus on light‑infrastructure with outsized returns (e.g., lab setups, boreholes, clinic equipment). Large builds are done only with co‑funders.

How are programs measured?

Each program has quarterly KPIs — attendance, outcomes (e.g., pass rates, referrals completed), and cost‑per‑beneficiary. We publish summaries annually.

Can companies sponsor a region‑wide rollout?

Yes. We offer co‑branded rollouts with reporting and storytelling support. Start via the form above.
