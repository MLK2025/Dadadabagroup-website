FAQs

What crops do you prioritize?
High-value horticulture (tomato, pepper, leafy greens, berries), plus maize/rice pilots where irrigation economics are favorable.
How do investors participate?
Equity at project/cluster SPVs, JV structures, or yield-linked instruments with offtake security and ESG reporting.
How is impact measured?
Yield uplift, water productivity, farmer income, jobs, and GHG/soil carbon where applicable (with third-party audits).
