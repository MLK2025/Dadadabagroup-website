Investors

Dadaba Entertainment

Africa-born content and live experiences with global distribution. This page summarizes our thesis, model, traction and governance for investment partners.

Download investor overview Book a call

Investment thesis

We aggregate IP across film, music and digital, monetize through multi-window distribution and live, and compound value via brand and licensing.

Demand

Global appetite for African stories

Streamers and broadcasters seek distinctive voices; diaspora and youth audiences drive cross-border demand.

Supply

Creator pipeline & studio services

Low-to-mid budget slates with disciplined greenlighting; in-house production services protect margins.

Monetization

Diverse revenue stack

Upfront commissioning, box office, touring, music royalties, ads/AVOD, sponsorships, and IP licensing.

Business model & unit economics

We prioritize repeatable formats, strong recoup waterfalls and transparent reporting.

Slate strategy

Risk-balanced portfolio

Mix of commissioned series (cash-flow efficient), co-productions (rights retention), and low-capex digital originals (high frequency).

Unit economics (illustrative)

For a mid-budget series

~X% margin on service/commission; backend uplift via format sales, soundtrack, brand extensions and FAST windows.

Cash discipline

Controls & reporting

Milestone-based spends, vendor standards, rights tracking, and investor dashboards with quarterly reviews.
